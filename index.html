Visit Beautiful Scenery of Bac Lieu


Bac Lieu is a coastal province in the Mekong Delta region of Vietnam, known for its rich cultural heritage and beautiful landscapes. The province is famous for its traditional music, particularly the "Don Ca Tai Tu" genre, and its scenic beauty, including the vast rice fields, rivers, and coastal areas.

Top Attractions

  • Vinh Hung Pagoda: A beautiful pagoda known for its unique architecture and serene atmosphere.
  • Bac Lieu Bird Sanctuary: A haven for bird watchers, featuring a variety of bird species in their natural habitat.
  • Ho Nam Ecotourism Area: A popular spot for eco-tourism, offering boat rides and opportunities to explore the local flora and fauna.
  • Coastal Beaches: Bac Lieu has several beautiful beaches, perfect for relaxation and enjoying the sea breeze.

House of the Prince of Bac Lieu


The House of the Prince of Bac Lieu is a historical site that showcases the life of the wealthy aristocrat, Tran Trinh Huy, who was known for his extravagant lifestyle and contributions to the local culture. The house features traditional architecture and is a testament to the opulence of the past.

Local Cuisine

Bac Lieu is also famous for its delicious cuisine, which includes dishes like "Banh Xeo" (Vietnamese pancakes), "Hu Tieu" (noodle soup), and fresh seafood. The local markets are a great place to sample these dishes and experience the vibrant food culture.

Getting There

Bac Lieu can be reached by road from major cities in the Mekong Delta, such as Can Tho and Soc Trang. The province is well-connected by bus and offers various transportation options for visitors.

Conclusion

Bac Lieu is a hidden gem in Vietnam, offering a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. Whether you're interested in exploring its historical sites, enjoying its cuisine, or simply relaxing by the beach, Bac Lieu has something for everyone.
